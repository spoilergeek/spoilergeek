GreenArrowTV’s Arrow Spoilers Page

Now, it’s time for an Arrow spoilers page on GreenArrowTV! The purpose of this page is to collect known spoilers for the Arrow television series airing in the United States on The CW, but keep in mind that plans and details sometimes change. GreenArrowTV never intends to ruin the show by having a spoilers page. When new spoilers are known, we’ll try to update this site as often as possible. Please do not reproduce spoilers and content from GreenArrowTV; instead, just place a link to the site.

Episode #6.1: “Fallout”

Written By: Marc Guggenheim & Wendy Mericle
Directed By: James Bamford
Guest Stars: Manu Bennett (Slade Wilson), Jack Moore (William)
Air Date: Thursday, October 12, 2017

NEW! Some official photos from the Arrow season premiere are here!

The CW’s Official Description: THE AFTERMATH — Oliver (Stephen Amell) deals with the fallout of the explosion on the island. James Bamford directed the episode written by Marc Guggenheim & Wendy Mericle (#601).  Original airdate 10/12/2017.

– Realistically, it looks like Samantha Clayton might be the only one to NOT make it off the island alive. RIP Babymama.
– William appears in this episode and shares a scene with Oliver where he blames him for what happened. Stephen’s first day of shooting Season 6 was doing scenes with William.
– Five months will have passed since Season 5, just like real time. There will be flashbacks to what happened just before and after the end of the Arrow Season 5 finale “Lian Yu.”
– At some point, Slade tells Oliver “we walk in two worlds. We can only do that for so long.”
– Black Siren obviously lives. “We wouldn’t bring her back just to kill her again,” Marc Guggenheim told a fan.
– The new Oliver Queen voiceover is so spoilery that Stephen Amell could not do it at a recent convention.

The Flash #4.5: “Girls  Night Out”

Air Date: November 2017

– Felicity Smoak (Emily Bett Rickards) appears in the fifth episode of The Flash Season 4 which tells us she, at least, will be alive next season.

Season 6 Spoilers

– Raisa the Maid (Kathleen Gati) is returning in Season 6. She has not appeared on Arrow since the first season.
– Hope for Olicity? Marc Guggenheim has said those two were set on a “specific trajectory” at the end of Season 5, which sounds positive for fans of the pairing.
– There are multiple “Big Bads” this year, and one of them will be played by Person of Interest’s Michael Emerson. We have no idea who he will be playing.
– Kirk Acevedo will be playing Ricardo Diaz a.k.a. Richard Dragon.
– At a convention in Portland, Stephen Amell revealed “two truths and a lie” about Season 6. One was that there is a single Big Bad this year; two is that there will be a giant rift between Oliver and one of the original team members. Three is that Oliver’s identity as Green Arrow is revealed. We know Choice #1 is not true, so that means #2 and #3 are most likely happening.
– There are some reports that David Ramsey has said that there will be some people getting married in Season 6. Oliver & Felicity?
– Katie Cassidy will be back as a SERIES REGULAR for Season 6, but as Black Siren!
– Though Thea did supposedly survive, it sounds like she hasn’t filmed much for Season 6 as of yet.
– Sydelle Noel has been cast in the recurring role of FBI agent Samanda Watson for Arrow Season 6.
– The Arrow portion of the four-show crossover will air in a special Monday night time slot on November 29.
– John Barrowman would like to return as Malcolm.
– Colton Haynes says that Roy Harper will be returning “very soon.”
– The producers already have an idea of where they’d want to go for Season 7.
